Abusive

Abusive is an adjective that refers to behavior or actions that are cruel, harmful, or insulting, often directed toward others.

Example: The coach was criticized for his abusive behavior toward the players. Example: She escaped an abusive relationship and found safety at a shelter. Example: His abusive language during the argument shocked everyone present.

Summary:

Abusive describes behavior that involves mistreatment, cruelty, or harmful actions, especially toward others, often in a verbal, emotional, or physical manner.

Related Words:

Cruel, Hurtful, Hostile, Offensive

Antonyms:

Kind, Compassionate, Respectful, Gentle
